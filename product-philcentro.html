PhilCentro

Client Project

Centro, powered by Philcentro Technologies, Inc., is a pioneering platform revolutionizing infrastructure management. With a deep commitment to innovation, accountability, and progress, we stand at the forefront of transforming how Local Government Units (LGUs) and stakeholders oversee infrastructure projects. Backed by cutting-edge technology and a passion for community development, Centro is more than a platform; it's a movement that empowers LGUs to build a brighter future.

Application

The PhilCentro Application is a project management and infrastructure monitoring application designed for Local Government Units (LGUs) and construction stakeholders. The platform provides real-time project monitoring, data analytics, collaboration tools, and funding support.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • User Interface Design: Created high-fidelity wireframes, designed components, made responsive designs for varying platforms (desktop, tablet, and mobile), and ensured alignment with the design goals.
  • Prototype Development: Built an interactive prototype that can simulate the final product.
  • Collaboration: Worked closely with another UI/UX designer who liaised with the product manager and developers, functioning as a junior designer within the team.
  • Asset Management: Maintained design assets to ensure they are easily accessible and up-to-date; and created a style guide to assist the developers with developing the application.
  • Branding and Visual Elements: Developed the color palette, typography, and other key design elements that aligns with the brandâ€™s identity.
  • Graphic Design: Designed marketing materials for the company: Calling cards, letter heads, and brochures.
