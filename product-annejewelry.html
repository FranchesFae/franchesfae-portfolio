Anne Jewelry

Independent Project

Anne Jewelry is a small, independently run jewelry brand inspired by Anne of Green Gables. Creating classic pieces that capture the charm and spirit of Anne’s world.

Website

The Anne Jewelry Website is an e-commerce platform that sells jewelry inspired by the Anne of Green Gables series. The website combines storybook aesthetics with classic designs, offering a unique shopping experience.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • User Interface Design: Created high-fidelity wireframes, designed components, and ensured alignment with the design goals.
  • Prototype Development: Built an interactive prototype that can simulate the final product.
  • Asset Management: Maintained design assets to ensure they are easily accessible and up-to-date.
  • Branding and Visual Elements: Developed the logo, color palette, typography, and other key design elements that aligns with the brand’s identity.
