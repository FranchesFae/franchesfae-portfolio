AsianLink.AI

Client Project

AsianLink.AI is your connect to news, public service, and e-commerce. It is an emerging global advanced internet technology company powered by artificial intelligence (AI) that aims to level the playing field for the global Filipino and improve the quality of life around the world through information empowerment, better public service delivery, and innovative access to products and business services.

Website

The AsianLink.AI Website is designed to showcase the mobile application. It provides background information about the app’s features, the team behind it, and the overall problems that the application can solve.

Mobile Application

The AsianLink.AI Mobile App integrates public services, news, and e-commerce all in one platform. It offers a range of features designed to support filipinos in their daily lives.

ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

  • User Interface Design: Created high-fidelity wireframes, designed components, made responsive designs for varying platforms (desktop, tablet, and mobile), and ensured alignment with the design goals.
  • Prototype Development: Built an interactive prototype that can simulate the final product.
  • Quality Assurance Testing: Ensured the final product matches the prototype.
  • Collaboration: Worked closely with another UI/UX designer who liaised with the product manager and developers, functioning as a junior designer within the team.
  • Asset Management: Maintained design assets to ensure they are easily accessible and up-to-date; and created a style guide to assist the developers with developing the application.
  • Branding and Visual Elements: Developed the color palette, typography, and other key design elements that aligns with the brand’s identity
  • Graphic Design: Designed standees that will help showcase the AsianLink.AI mobile application.
